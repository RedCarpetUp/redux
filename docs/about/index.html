We lend where banks cannot see

RedCarpet lends to customers in India to finance their online purchases - ecommerce, travel etc. In India, credit and finance companies are able to service less than 3% of the customer base because there is no data, no widespread credit bureaus to profile and score customers. RedCarpet gathers data from the field (and other partners). We use this data to service customers who never had any access to credit before. Our design philosophy at RedCarpet is called "Design for Honesty". We are acutely aware of the responsibility we have to safeguard our customer data.
